Project Description

This project is designed to secure a cybersecurity internship by December 2025 through a comprehensive strategy that combines professional credential attainment, portfolio development, and direct employer engagement.

Unlike traditional portfolio projects that end with website deployment, this initiative extends through the entire internship application cycle. The project includes earning the CompTIA Security+ certification, building a professional portfolio showcasing hands-on cybersecurity projects, submitting 15+ internship applications, attending career fairs, and conducting informational interviews with industry professionals.

Success is measured not by artifacts created, but by tangible outcomes: receiving at least one internship offer or securing three or more second-round interviews. Every deliverable directly supports employer engagement, ensuring that preparation translates into real career opportunities.
